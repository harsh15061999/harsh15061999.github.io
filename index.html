Project 6

DEFUNGI IMAGE CLASSIFICATION

The DEFUNGI Image Classification project is a machine learning initiative aimed at accurately classifying various types of fungi from image data. This project leverages advanced deep learning techniques, specifically convolutional neural networks (CNNs), to analyze and categorize images of fungi into distinct classes. By utilizing a comprehensive dataset of fungi images, the model is trained to recognize and differentiate between species with high accuracy. The project aims to support researchers, mycologists, and enthusiasts in identifying fungi species efficiently, contributing to studies in ecology, biodiversity, and conservation. The code, data preprocessing steps, model architecture, and training procedures are documented to ensure reproducibility and facilitate further improvements and applications.
